Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Everyday Senior Resources ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our Facebook advertisements and lead forms.

1. Information We Collect

When you voluntarily submit our form, we collect the personal information you provide, which may include your:

  • First and Last Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Responses to survey questions (such as Medicare status or areas where you need financial assistance)

2. How We Use Your Information

We collect this information strictly to provide the service or information you requested. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Verify your eligibility for senior benefit programs, allowances, or resources in your zip code.
  • Contact you via phone call, text message (SMS), or email to deliver your results and provide assistance.
  • Improve our community resources and outreach efforts.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to unauthorized third parties. To fulfill your request, we may share your information securely with trusted licensed specialists, agents, or community partners who will contact you directly to explain your eligible benefits.

4. Consent to Contact

By providing your phone number and email address, you explicitly consent to be contacted by Everyday Senior Resources and our trusted partners via phone calls, text messages, and emails regarding your benefit inquiry, even if you are on a state or national Do Not Call list. Standard message and data rates may apply.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate and standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
